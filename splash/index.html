About Me

Jamar Nelson, also known as DjAquafina, is a native of Greenville, South Carolina who has developed a range of skills to help him thrive as an artist. Not only is he a skilled DJ on the weekends, but he is also a talented producer, audio engineer, and graphic artist. His production skills include composing, arranging, and recording melodies, harmonies, and rhythms, while his mixing skills involve balancing levels and adding effects to create a cohesive final product. In addition, his mastering skills involve adjusting the overall volume and EQ of a track to meet industry standards and make it sound professional. Overall, Jamar Nelson is a versatile artist with a range of skills that allow him to excel in multiple areas of the music industry.

Having these skills has given me greater control over the creative process and the ability to produce music that sounds exactly how I envision it. It has also made me more competitive in the music industry, as many jobs in the field require knowledge of production and mixing techniques. Additionally, being able to produce and mix my own music has saved me time and money by eliminating the need to hire outside professionals for these tasks.

I have gained my knowledge in these areas through a combination of online tutorials, college courses, workshops, and hands-on experience. I also have access to good quality equipment and software, and have sought guidance and feedback from experienced professionals. Through practice and experimentation, I have continued to improve my skills and develop my own unique style. I am confident in my ability to produce, mix, and master music to a high standard.
